Company Support Knowledge Base

Order Policy:
Orders are processed within 24 hours.
Delivery takes 3 to 5 business days.
Delays may occur during holidays.

Refund Policy:
Refunds are issued within 7 business days after approval.
Refunds are processed to the original payment method.
Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery.

Escalation Policy:
Issues involving payments or legal matters must be escalated.
Customers requesting managers should be escalated.
